This means that you can convert between fractions, decimals, and percentages. You can determine if a decimal terminates or repeats. You can solve multi-step word problems by converting between forms.

Glossary

Decimal
A number that has a decimal point followed by digits that show the fractional part.

0.01 (one hundreth)
-45.6 (negative forty-five and six tenths)


Fraction
A number that represents a part of a whole.

2/5 (two fifths)


Percentage
Parts per 100. The symbol is %.

25% means 25 per 100.
